Sponsor Kegiatan Penyelenggara Kegiatan Tentang Sofund
  • Why do social activities projects need to be supported?
    Social-based activities promise a broad impact for a group of people or more and provide a domino effect that can ignite developments and advancements in the field of HR and other fields.
  • Why will the sofund be profitable for the company?
    Companies will have the opportunity to choose social projects that will be given financial support through a single platform, where this can increase the effectiveness of time, facilitated by the categorization of social activities based on the sub-types of activities.
  • How much does the sofund platform use for the activity sponsor?
    We do not have the right to request funds from the sponsor of the activity, but if the campaign of a social activity is successful, thus the sofund will reduce 5% of the total funds received by the fundraiser.
  • Who can apply for sponsorship on sofund?
    Students, NGOs, communities, or organizers of activities that are at least 17 years old and have an identity card when submitting funds.
  • How many can be funded from the proposal for organizing activities?
    Sofund does not specify the minimum or maximum amount of funds that can be funded. Even so, the provisions regarding counterpart packages will be determined by the organizer of the activity.
  • How does the systematic sponsor of the activity provide support on sofund?
    First, prospective event sponsors can register first on the sofund platform. After that, the company can choose social activities that can be given support for activities that have submitted proposals. If appropriate, the activity sponsor can choose the amount of funds to be provided and the funds can then be sent through an intermediary sofund.
  • What types of activities can be campaigned on sofund?
    Sofund has the aim of being able to provide the maximum benefit to all Indonesian people. Based on this, sofund limits the activities that can be campaigned on this platform specifically for social activities, affecting groups / communities / communities, which are non-individualistic and inclusive.
  • How many activity sponsors can the proposal be submitted by the organization of the activity?
    The maximum number of companies that can be asked for sponsors by the event organizers is three sponsors. However, any proposal rejected by the activity sponsor can be replaced by the activity organizer to submit the proposal in the sponsor of another activity according to the number of rejected proposals.
  • How systematically do event organizers campaign on sofund?
    First, the organizer of the activity first registers the activity account that will be campaigned on sofund. After that, the organizer of the activity can upload a proposal to the sponsor of the activity. If approved, the sponsor can be searched according to the agreed amount.
  • What is crowdfunding?
    Crowdfunding is the practice of raising funds together from various actors in assisting funding carried out by an actor, both social and non-social.
  • What is SocialFunding (sofund)?
    Sofund is an online-based crowdfunding platform that bridges between the organizers of social-based activities (organizers of activities) with the party providing financial support from the company (sponsor of activities) with priority to both parties.
